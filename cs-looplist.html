Looplist

A social-first, low-friction shopping and curation platform.

We used complex artificial intelligence and machine learning, to create a personalized and data-driven recommendation engine that was then presented through a very visual and interactive mobile app. It included finding, sharing, collecting and liking products, purchasing products, and adding your own products.
